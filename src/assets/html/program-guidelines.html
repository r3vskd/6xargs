Scope Exclusions

Disclosure of Client/Organization Information

Information disclosed by a client/organization in 6xargs; These reports will be closed as informational and we encourage you to report the disclosure to the relevant program.

Misleading AI Information

Manipulation of AI to produce misleading or false information. We continually work to improve the accuracy of responses.

Header Validation and URL Issues

Reports targeting lack of header validation or exploiting multiple forward slashes in the root path segment of a URL.

User Enumeration

Reports that list already claimed users and program handlers. This does not reveal sensitive information, regardless of whether the associated profiles are public or private.

Badges Functioinality

The ability to manipulate badge functionality by closing submissions reported by yourself or a team member in a verified program.
