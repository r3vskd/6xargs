Privacy Policy

At 6xargs, we value and protect your data and privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our bug bounty and cybersecurity services:

  • Personal identification information (nickname, email, password)
  • Technical information about reports generated by hackers regarding vulnerabilities they find.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our bug bounty services
  • Improve and personalize the user experience
  • Process reward payments
  • Communicate with you about service updates and offers
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

3. Data Protection (ARCO and LFPDPPP Compliance)

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Continuous security monitoring and regular audits
  • Privacy and security training for our staff

LFPDPPP Compliance

Our Platform is committed to privacy and protection of personal data, complying with the Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (LFPDPPP), which regulates the processing of personal data in Mexico.

Under this law:

  • Personal data collected (email, nickname, and password) is used only for the purposes indicated in this policy.
  • Users have the right to exercise their ARCO Rights (Access, Rectification, Cancellation, and Opposition) at any time by sending a request through our contact email.
  • Appropriate security measures are implemented to protect data against loss, alteration, destruction, or unauthorized access.
  • Data will only be transferred to third parties when strictly necessary to fulfill service obligations or in cases provided by law.

ARCO Rights

According to the LFPDPPP, users can:

  • Access: Know what personal data we have and how we process it.
  • Rectify: Request corrections if your data is inaccurate or outdated.
  • Cancel: Request the deletion of your data when it is no longer necessary for the indicated purposes.
  • Oppose: Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.

Requests related to the exercise of these rights can be sent to: [correo@tuempresa.com].

4. Information Handling

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With organizations participating in bug bounty programs, limited to information necessary for vulnerability verification
  • With service providers who help us operate our platform
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information. If you disagree, you can freely reject the clause and leave at any time. Here are your privacy rights:

  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to rectify inaccurate information
  • Right to delete your information (with certain limitations)
  • Right to object to the processing of your information
  • Right to data portability

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve the user experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may periodically update this privacy policy. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "last updated" date.

8. Confidentiality

  • Security protocols (encryption, firewalls, access controls) are implemented to protect all personal data and information generated on the Platform.
  • Data is stored on secure servers, and periodic backups are performed to prevent loss or unauthorized access.
  • The confidentiality of vulnerability reports is essential; both hackers and companies agree to maintain the discretion of the exchanged information.
