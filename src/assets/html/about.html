Our Vision

At 6xargs, we envision a digital future where security is not a barrier, but a catalyst for innovation. We believe in a safer internet, where companies can grow and users can browse with confidence.

Our mission is to revolutionize cybersecurity through the seamless fusion of advanced artificial intelligence and human capabilities. This synergy allows us to anticipate, identify and mitigate cyber threats at unprecedented speed and scale.

AI + Hacking

AI is the engine of the 4th phase of the industrial revolution, so we create the world first Security AI ready for enterprises.

By combining the power of artificial intelligence with the expertise of security researchers, we're able to identify vulnerabilities faster and more efficiently than traditional methods.

Ethics

At 6xargs, ethics are at the heart of everything we do. We recognize the immense responsibility that comes with handling sensitive information and developing powerful technologies.

Our commitment to ethics is manifested in:

The API

Our Agentic-AI API for Hacking connects security experts with your platform to identify and resolve critical vulnerabilities.

With our API, you can:

  • Integrate our AI agent into your existing bug bounty program
  • Receive real-time notifications on new vulnerabilities
  • Access a vast database of vulnerability information

Vulnerability Management

With our vulnerability management API, you can:

  • Acceder a múltiples plataformas de bug bounty y pentesting como Hack The Box, TryHackMe y otras desde una única interfaz CLI
  • Ejecutar comandos de pentesting directamente desde la web sin necesidad de configurar entornos locales
  • Gestionar y hacer seguimiento de vulnerabilidades encontradas en todas las plataformas de forma centralizada

Added Value with AI

Nuestra API potenciada con IA ofrece ventajas únicas para profesionales de ciberseguridad:

  • Automatización inteligente de tareas repetitivas en plataformas como Hack The Box y TryHackMe mediante comandos CLI
  • Recomendaciones personalizadas de técnicas de pentesting basadas en el contexto específico de cada plataforma
  • Análisis predictivo de vulnerabilidades que combina datos de múltiples plataformas para identificar patrones de seguridad emergentes
